About

I am a PhD candidate in Computational Neuroscience at Universitat de Girona (UdG). I started my Neuroscience reserach at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF), Barcelona, Spain. My research investigates the computational foundations of consciousness through formal models of meditation, sleep, and attentional dynamics.

My work bridges contemplative neurophenomenology and computational theory, developing hierarchical frameworks like "Thoughtseeds" that formalize mental processes using active inference, nested Markov blankets, and agentic architectures. Recent publications explore conscious entry into sleep (Yoga Nidra), thought dynamics in meditative states, and computational models of expert-novice differences in focused attention meditation.

Beyond academia, I have 15+ years of industry experience leading AI/ML and data engineering initiatives across banking, healthcare, telecom, and retail. As Principal Consultant at Samadhi.ai, I build provenance-first GraphRAG pipelines, multi-agent orchestration systems, and neurotech feasibility studies integrating sleep wearables with cloud platforms.

I hold an MS in Brain and Cognition from UPF, Spain. MBA from the Asian Institute of Management (ADB Scholar), Philippines and BE in Computers from NIST University, India. My technical expertise spans deep learning, generative AI, Bayesian modeling, and distributed data systems on hyperscalers like AWS.
