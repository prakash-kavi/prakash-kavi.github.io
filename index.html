About

I am a PhD candidate in Computational Neuroscience at Universitat de Girona (UdG) and began my neuroscience research at Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF), Barcelona. My research investigates the computational foundations of "content of consciousness" through formal models of meditation, sleep, and attentional dynamics.

As an AI/ML architect, I design end-to-end systems that connect data, models, evaluation, and deployment. My work emphasizes provenance, reliability, and human-centered decision support for research and enterprise use cases.

I bring 15+ years of industry experience across healthcare, finance, and enterprise systems. I hold an MS in Brain and Cognition (UPF), Spain, an MBA from the Asian Institute of Management (ADB Scholar), Philippines, and a BE in Computer Science from NIST University, India.
